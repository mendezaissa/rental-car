Rental Requirements:


WEEKEND RENTALS

All Saturday reservations require a 2 day rental minimum as HTX offices are closed on Sundays.

DRIVERS LICENSE

A valid government-issued driver's license will be required of all drivers at the time of rental. The license may not be expired, cracked, broken, or temporary permit/license. International customers will be required to provide a passport, international drivers license and proof of insurance.

MINIMUM DRIVING AGE

All drivers must be 25 years or older.

INSURANCE & PAYMENT REQUIREMENTS

All renters are required to provide proof of full coverage liability insurance. We do not sell insurance coverage for our rentals. If you do not have full coverage verifiable insurance, we cannot rent a vehicle to you. We do accept certain credit card rental insurance which incurs a larger security damage deposit. All renters must have a valid drivers license, valid credit card for security damage deposit, and verifiable auto insurance with a minimum amounts in property damage coverage. Debit cards are accepted, but the deposit may be held longer after return.
